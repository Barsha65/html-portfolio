The Best K-dramas according to Barsha

My Top 4 web series of all-time

I'm Not a Robot

Really a nice rom-com story and it highlights the importance of trust among individuals

True Beauty

A high school romcom whic focuses on a girl low esteem due to her looks which she overcome with her career as makeup artist and also the second lead syndrome.

My name

A revenge with lots of suspense K-drama in which a daughter seeks revenge for his father

The Penthouse

Really worth watching all the seasons if you like revenge and suspense
